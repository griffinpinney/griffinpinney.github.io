It's a little sad to me that this kind of puzzle, which was once a fun challenge, can now be one-shotted with AI. Of course, one doesn't need to use AI, but I can't help but feel that we, as humans, are being robbed of something genuinely valuable just by the fact that this is now possible. Going forward, I hope Jane Street will take care to write more of the kinds of puzzles that we're still better at solving than AI.
